# Legends: Ad Copy

Expert frameworks and methodologies that inform advertising copy.

## Primary Experts

### David Ogilvy
**Framework:** Image and headline testing
**Key Concepts:**
- Headlines are 80% of ad effectiveness
- Promise a benefit in headline
- Be specific with numbers
- Long copy sells

**How we apply it:**
Test multiple headlines, lead with benefits, use specific claims.

---

### Claude Hopkins
**Framework:** Scientific Advertising
**Key Concepts:**
- Test everything
- Use coupons/tracking
- Specificity sells
- Tell stories

**How we apply it:**
Create multiple variations for testing, include specific numbers and claims.

---

### Gary Halbert
**Framework:** A-pile vs B-pile
**Key Concepts:**
- Your ad competes for attention
- Starving crowd principle
- Emotional hooks
- Urgency

**How we apply it:**
Create hooks that stop the scroll, tap into emotions, create legitimate urgency.

## Copy Formulas

### PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
1. State the problem
2. Agitate the pain
3. Present your solution

### AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
1. Grab attention
2. Build interest
3. Create desire
4. Call to action

### 4Ps (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push)
1. Make a promise
2. Paint a picture
3. Provide proof
4. Push to action
