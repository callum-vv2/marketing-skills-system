# Legends: Case Study

Expert frameworks and methodologies that inform customer success stories.

## Primary Frameworks

### STAR Method
**Framework:** Situation, Task, Action, Result
**Key Concepts:**
- Situation: Context and background
- Task: The challenge or goal
- Action: What was done (your solution)
- Result: Measurable outcomes

**How we apply it:**
Structure every case study with clear STAR sections to make the transformation obvious.

---

### StoryBrand (Donald Miller)
**Framework:** Customer as Hero
**Key Concepts:**
- Customer is the hero, not your product
- Your product is the guide
- Focus on transformation, not features

**How we apply it:**
Write case studies that celebrate the customer's success, not just your product's features.

---

### Before/After/Bridge (BAB)
**Framework:** Transformation narrative
**Key Concepts:**
- Before: The painful state
- After: The desired state achieved
- Bridge: How they got there (your solution)

**How we apply it:**
Create emotional resonance by vividly describing the before and after states.
