## Readability Standards

### Flesch-Kincaid
- Target: 60-70 score
- Grade level: 6-8
- Short sentences
- Common words

### Hemingway App Principles
- Grade 6 reading level
- Highlight complex sentences
- Flag passive voice
- Remove adverbs

---

## Conversion Copy Checklist

### Clarity
- [ ] One idea per paragraph
- [ ] Jargon removed or explained
- [ ] Benefit before feature
- [ ] Reader knows what to do next

### Credibility
- [ ] Specific claims backed up
- [ ] Social proof included
- [ ] Credentials established

### Compelling
- [ ] Emotional hook
- [ ] Stakes established
- [ ] Urgency (if appropriate)
- [ ] CTA clear and actionable
