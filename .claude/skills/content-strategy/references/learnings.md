# Content Strategy Learnings

> Insights captured from content strategy execution. These learnings compound over time to improve future strategy.

---

## How to Use This File

After executing content strategy or reviewing performance:

1. **Document what worked** - Content pillars, themes, or calendars that drove results
2. **Document what didn't work** - Strategies that underperformed expectations
3. **Note audience insights** - What you learned about content preferences
4. **Capture timing insights** - When content performs best
5. **Record format insights** - Which formats resonate with which audiences

Use the templates below to maintain consistency.
