## Sales-Led (SLG) Examples

### Gong.io

**Hero:**
- **Super-header:** "Revenue Intelligence Platform"
- **Headline:** "Unlock reality to hit your number"
- **Sub-headline:** "Gong captures your customer interactions, then delivers insights at scale, empowering your team to make decisions based on data instead of opinions."
- **CTA:** "Get a Demo"

**Why it works:**
- Outcome-focused headline ("hit your number")
- Clear category in super-header
- Sub-headline explains the how
- Single high-intent CTA

## Hero Formula Examples

### What is it? + What's in it for me? + How do I get it?

**Stripe:**
- What: "Payments infrastructure for the internet"
- For me: "Millions of businesses... build products, create subscriptions, send invoices..."
- How: "Start now" / "Contact sales"

**Slack:**
- What: "Slack is your productivity platform"
- For me: "...connects you to the people and apps you work with..."
- How: "Try for free"

**Zoom:**
- What: "Video communications platform"
- For me: "...empowers you to accomplish more"
- How: "Sign up free"
